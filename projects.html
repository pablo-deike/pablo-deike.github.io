Deepseek VSCode extension

Deepseek has shocked the AI world when they released their Deepseek-r1 model. This is one of the cheapest and most efficient models on the market, and has the advantage of being open source, so everyone can run it as long as they have the required hardware. In my case, I've created an easy vscode extension, for personal use mainly, that connects locally to the Deepseek-r1 small model (available in llama) and one can access this AI assistant from VSCode without any internet connection (ideal for long trips)

Barnes Hut simulation

Academical group where we developed an imnplementation of the barnes hut algorithm in C++. This algorithm is applied to a many body system, in this case of particles being attracted by the gravitational force. This algorithm reduces the number of calculations from $O(N^2)$ to $O(NlogN)$, where N is the number of particles affected by gravity, by making smart approximations using the center of mass and some optimizable parameter theta.

Fun projects

Prisonner's dilemma

As a board game enthusiast, one of my favourite math theories is game theory. Therefore, I created a simple repository about the prisonner's dilemma, one of the classic problems in game theory. Two prisoners must independently decide whether to cooperate or betray the other, without knowing what the other will choose. Their choices determine their fate, creating a scenario where rational self-interest can lead to worse outcomes for both. In this repository I have created some simple strategies to play the game and see what has the best outcome on average (who has the most points)
